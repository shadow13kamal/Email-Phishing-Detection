How It Works

1

Text Preprocessing

Email text is lowercased, stripped of punctuation, tokenized, and filtered for stop words using NLTK.

2

TF-IDF Features

Preprocessed text is converted into numeric feature vectors using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency.

3

ML Classification

Three models are trained and compared. The best-performing model classifies the email as Phishing or Safe.

Key Features

Phishing vs. Safe classification with confidence score

Phishing keyword identification and highlighting
